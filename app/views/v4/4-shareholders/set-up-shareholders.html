The company must have at least one shareholder.

Each shareholder must have at least one share.

What is a shareholder
Shareholders are the company owners. They own shares in the company, which entitle them to a share of company profits. Most shareholders also have a say in important company issues, like appointing new directors.

You can change the shareholders and their shares at any time after the company is registered by contacting Companies House.

The company may not have any PSCs if none of the shareholders own more than 25% of its shares. You'll still need to confirm this.
