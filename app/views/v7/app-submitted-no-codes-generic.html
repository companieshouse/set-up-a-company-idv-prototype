If you did not provide a Companies House personal code for any PSCs

You should notify those PSCs that they must:

  • verify their identity for Companies House, if they have not done so already
  • provide us with their Companies House personal code, so that their verified identity can be linked to our records

Individual PSCs must do this within 14 days of the company's incorporation date.

If any of the PSCs is a relevant legal entity (RLE), the RLE must provide its relevant officer's details within 28 days of the company's incorporation date.

! Warning If a PSC or RLE does not comply with identity verification requirements, they will be committing an offence and may have to pay a fine.
